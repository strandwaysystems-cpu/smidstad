About Tara

I'm not a fitness model.
I'm a fitness person.

I started posting about fitness in 2019 because I was frustrated by how unrealistic and unsustainable most fitness content was. I wanted to show what it actually looks like to be consistent — not perfect.

I'm based in Nashville, TN. I'm a NASM-certified personal trainer and a Precision Nutrition coach. I've built a community of 200K+ people who are done with diet culture and ready to build habits that last.

I work with brands that align with my values, and I take on a limited number of 1-on-1 coaching clients each quarter.
