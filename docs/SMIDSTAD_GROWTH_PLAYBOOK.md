## 1. Positioning

### 1.1 The one-liner
**Smidstad builds high-converting websites and done-for-you content for fitness coaches, personal trainers, and creators — live in days, not months.**

### 1.2 Who we serve (and who we don't)
- **In:** Fitness coaches, personal trainers, online coaches, and influencers of any kind (fitness, lifestyle, wellness, niche creators) with an engaged audience and a weak or non-existent web presence.
- **Out:** Local trades and home services — that's **Skerry Signal's** lane. The two brands never overlap niches.

### 1.3 The ideal client profile
- Audience of ~500–5,000+ engaged followers (Instagram, TikTok, or YouTube).
- Currently runs on a **Linktree, Carrd, or nothing** — no real home base.
- Sells a program, coaching package, course, or brand deal.
- Hates writing their own content (this is the upsell goldmine).
- Owner = decision-maker. No committee, no procurement.

### 1.4 Why creators are the right beachhead
- **Easiest niche to find** — they broadcast publicly; you can build a target list in an hour.
- **Digitally native** — comfortable paying by link, no "mail me an invoice" friction.
- **Built-in recurring upsell** — creators need content forever, so a one-off site becomes a monthly retainer.
- **Tightly networked** — one happy creator refers three more.
- **Brand fit** — Smidstad's "forge / digital craft" identity reads premium and modern to a creator audience.

### 1.5 The positioning wedge (what makes us different)
- **Speed:** live in days, while agencies take 4–8 weeks.
- **Fixed price, published up front:** no "request a quote," no sales-call gauntlet.
- **Creator-native:** we speak offers, launches, link-in-bio, and content — not "corporate web solutions."
- **Done-for-you content:** the website is the front door; the content engine is the relationship.

## 2. The Offer Ladder (Finalized)

Three fixed-price tiers. Prices published on the site so prospects self-qualify before contacting you. **Pro Site is the lead offer** — anchor everything to it.

| Tier | Name | Price (CAD) | Built For | What's Included | Delivery |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| **Entry** | **Starter Site** | **$1,500** | Coaches & influencers living on Instagram who need a real hub | 1–3 page conversion site (or premium link-in-bio): offer, social proof, booking/email-capture CTA, mobile-first, AI-written copy, basic SEO, 1 revision round | **3 business days** |
| **Core** ⭐ | **Pro Site** | **$2,500** | Trainers & creators ready to own their brand | 5-page creator site (Home, About, Programs/Services, Results/Testimonials, Contact), booking integration, local + on-page SEO, analytics, AI-written copy, 2 revision rounds, 30-day support | **5 business days** |
| **Premium** | **Brand + Content Engine** | **$4,500** | Scaling creators who want presence *and* consistency | Everything in Pro Site **plus** an AI content starter pack (8 IG captions or 4 long posts), brand voice guide, on-page SEO build-out, social-profile optimization, 3 revision rounds, 60-day support, optional Stripe/booking embed | **7–10 business days** |

### 2.1 The $99 paid mockup step (keep this)
Before a full build, offer a **$99 real preview** of their site — credited 100% toward any package. It qualifies serious buyers, filters tire-kickers, and turns "maybe" into a paid micro-commitment. Keep it as the bridge between interest and the full sale.

### 2.2 Add-ons (the real margin)
| Add-on | Price (CAD) | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| **Monthly content retainer** | **$400–$800 / mo** | 4–8 posts or captions. *The recurring revenue engine — pitch on every project.* |
| AI content starter pack | $400–$600 | 8 IG captions or 4 posts, same-week delivery |
| Social-profile / brand voice tune-up | $250 | Bio, highlights, positioning polish |
| Rush delivery (48h) | +$500 | For launch deadlines |
| Care plan (hosting + updates) | $75–$150 / mo | Recurring, low-touch |

### 2.3 Payment structure
- **Under $1,000:** 100% upfront.
- **$1,000–$5,000 (all three tiers):** **50% deposit via Stripe link to start, 50% on final approval.**
  - Starter deposit: **$750** · Pro deposit: **$1,250** · Authority deposit: **$2,250**
- No contract required to start — a 1-page scope doc + the intake form is enough.

## 4. The 30-Day Acquisition Calendar

| Day(s) | Phase | Action | Goal / Outcome |
|---|---|---|---|
| **0** | Setup | Complete all of §3: Stripe links, intake form, 2–3 spec mockups, site repositioned, form activated | Sales machine ready — "yes → paid → building" with zero friction |
| **1–2** | Build proof | Finalize spec mockups; capture clean screenshots and a shareable link for each | Ammunition ready to send in DMs |
| **1–2** | List build | Search Instagram/TikTok/FB for 30 creators (500–5k followers, Linktree/Carrd/no site). Log handle + 1 specific detail each | Cold target list of 30 |
| **3–5** | Personal network | DM every creator (or person who knows one) you already know. Use Script A | **First sale most likely here (3–10 days)** |
| **3–7** | Warm the cold list | Genuinely comment on 3–5 posts from each cold prospect over a few days | Recognition before the pitch |
| **5–10** | DM outreach | Begin Instagram DMs to warmed prospects using Script B. Target ~10 sends/day | 2–4 real conversations/day |
| **7–10** | Pitch & close | For every reply, send the relevant spec mockup → price → Stripe link (Script C/D) | First closes |
| **11–14** | Group value-posting | Post in 2–3 creator/coach FB groups (fitness, online coaching, influencer communities): a before/after of a spec site or a free tip ("3 things every coach's link-in-bio is missing") | Inbound DMs + credibility |
| **11–21** | Sustain + follow up | Keep 10 DMs/day; follow up with anyone who showed interest but didn't buy — send Stripe link on follow-up (Script E) | Converts ~20–30% of interested leads |
| **15–21** | Deliver | Build and ship first paid project(s) in 3–7 days | First delivered client |
| **21–30** | Flywheel | On delivery: request testimonial + screen-reaction, put it on the site, ask for a referral, pitch the monthly content retainer | Social proof live + first recurring revenue |

**Daily rhythm during outreach weeks:** 10 new DMs + reply to all conversations + comment on 5 prospect posts + 1 follow-up batch. ~45–60 min/day.

## 5. Spec-Mockup-First Pitch Scripts

> Rule: **never cold-pitch a price first.** Lead with the demo. Personalize the one bracketed detail — generic templates get ignored; specific outreach gets ~3× the reply rate.

### Script A — Personal network (warm)
> Hey [Name] — quick one. I just launched **Smidstad**, where I build slick websites + done-for-you content for coaches and creators. If you (or anyone you know) wants a landing page that actually converts followers into clients, I'd love to hook them up. Mind if I send you a quick example?

### Script B — Instagram cold DM (after engaging on 3–5 posts)
> Hey [Name] — been following your [program/content] and love what you're building. I design landing pages + content for coaches like you, and I actually put together a quick demo of how your site could look. Mind if I send it over? No strings — just think it'd level up how you present [their offer].

### Script C — The reveal (when they say "sure, send it")
> Here you go 👇 [mockup link/screenshots]
>
> Built this around [their offer/brand]. The idea: one clean page that turns your followers into booked clients — instead of a Linktree that just lists links. I can have the full thing live in [3–5] days. Want me to walk you through how it'd work?

### Script D — The close (when they're interested)
> Love it. Here's the deal — it's a fixed price, no surprises:
> **[Tier] — $[price] CAD**, live in **[X] business days**, includes [scope + revisions].
> To lock it in, here's the link (takes 2 min): **[Stripe deposit link]**. Once that's in, I'll send a quick 5-min intake form and start building today.

### Script E — Follow-up (interested but went quiet)
> Hey [Name] — still keen to get your site sorted? I've got a build slot opening up this week. Same fixed price, live in [X] days. Here's the link whenever you're ready: [Stripe link]. Happy to answer anything first.

### Script F — Facebook group value post (no pitch)
> 👋 I'm [Your Name] — I build websites + content for coaches and creators. Quick free tip: the 3 things almost every coach's link-in-bio is missing — (1) a single clear offer above the fold, (2) real client results, (3) one obvious "book now" button. Happy to take a quick look at anyone's setup and share what I'd change — just drop your link below. 🛠️

### Objection handlers
- **"How much?"** → "Fixed pricing, all on the site — Starter $1,500, Pro $2,500, full Brand + Content Engine $4,500 CAD. Want me to mock yours up first for $99, credited toward the build?"
- **"I already have a Linktree."** → "Totally — and it works for links. But it can't tell your story, show results, or capture emails. That's the gap a real page closes."
- **"Can you show me your work?"** → "Already did — that mockup is your site. That's exactly what you'd get, live in [X] days."
- **"Let me think about it."** → "Of course. I'll hold a build slot for you this week — here's the link if you decide to move: [Stripe link]."

## 7. Intake Form Checklist (Tally or Google Forms)

> Replaces the discovery call for the lower tiers. Keep it to one page, ~5 minutes.

**Fields to include:**
- [ ] Name, business/brand name, email, phone/IG handle
- [ ] What they sell (program/coaching/course/offer) + price point
- [ ] Their #1 goal for the site (bookings / email list / credibility / sales)
- [ ] Existing links (current site, Linktree, IG, TikTok, YouTube)
- [ ] Brand assets: logo, colors, fonts (upload), or "I need help with branding"
- [ ] 3–5 photos / headshots (upload)
- [ ] Testimonials / client results (paste or upload)
- [ ] Preferred booking tool (Calendly/Cal.com/none) + link
- [ ] Domain status (own it? need help?)
- [ ] Anything they love / hate in other creators' sites (1–2 example links)
- [ ] Tier purchased + add-ons (pre-filled or dropdown)

**Setup tasks:**
- [ ] Build the form in Tally (recommended) or Google Forms
- [ ] Enable file uploads for logo/photos
- [ ] Auto-email a copy to `hello@smidstad.com` on submission
- [ ] Add a friendly confirmation screen: "Got it — I'll start your build and be in touch within 24h."
- [ ] Paste the form URL into every Stripe link's post-payment redirect

## 8. The Flywheel (after client #1)

Each delivered client makes the next sale easier. On every delivery:

1. **Capture proof immediately** — request a testimonial + a short screen-recorded reaction (creators love being featured; their reactions are gold).
2. **Put it on the site** — now "See the work" is real, and "can you show me your work?" disappears as an objection.
3. **Ask for a referral** — "Know two other coaches who'd want this?" Creators are tightly networked.
4. **Pitch the retainer** — convert the one-off project into $400–800/mo recurring content. A client on a retainer is worth 5–10× a one-and-done.
