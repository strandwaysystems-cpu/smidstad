# Smidstad Mockup Spec Sheet
### The one-page recipe every creator/coach site is built and judged against
*Distilled from `SMIDSTAD_DESIGN_RESEARCH.md`. Use this to BUILD a mockup and to PASS/FAIL one before it goes to a prospect.*

---

## 0. How to use this sheet

1. **Identify the sub-niche** (fitness / coach / creator) — the design changes by niche.
2. **Build to the section order + the niche style row.**
3. **Run the Pass/Fail checklist.** If a mockup fails ANY non-negotiable, fix before sending.

> The whole point: don't redesign from scratch each time. Build one "golden template" per niche, then swap in each client's photos + copy.

---

## 1. The 8 NON-NEGOTIABLES (pass/fail every mockup)

A mockup may not go to a prospect unless it passes all 8:

- [ ] **Client-first hero.** First 5 seconds name the visitor's problem/outcome — NOT "Hi, I'm [Name]."
- [ ] **Mobile-first.** Designed phone-first; tested in the Instagram in-app browser; tap targets ≥48px; loads < 2.5s. (91–94% of traffic is mobile.)
- [ ] **Social proof distributed**, not siloed — next to every offer, above every CTA, plus a credibility bar. Not hidden on one page.
- [ ] **Authentic photography only.** Real photos of the coach/creator. Zero stock.
- [ ] **Frictionless primary action.** Booking calendar embedded (fitness) or qualifying application form (coach) — not just a contact form.
- [ ] **CTA every 2–3 scroll sections**, in a contrasting accent color. One primary CTA per page.
- [ ] **≤ 5 nav items.** More creates decision paralysis.
- [ ] **Email capture exists.** A lead magnet opt-in (social followers are rented; email is owned).

---

## 2. The proven homepage section order (works across all niches)

1. **Hero** — outcome headline + subheadline (who you help) + 1 CTA + real photo/looping video
2. **Credibility bar** — "as seen in" logos / certifications / key metrics ("500+ clients")
3. **Problem statement** — mirror the client's pain in their language
4. **Transformation** — before/after, outcomes, "what life looks like after"
5. **How it works** — simple 3 steps
6. **Social proof** — 2–3 testimonials w/ name, photo, specific result
7. **About teaser** — short story, link to full About
8. **Services** — 1–3 offers, each with its own CTA
9. **Lead magnet opt-in** — free resource + email capture
10. **Final CTA** — repeat the primary action

## 3. Design style by sub-niche (match the look to the buyer)

| Sub-niche | Look | Colors | Typography | Photography |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| **Fitness coach / trainer** | Bold, energetic, high-contrast | Black + white + **1 vibrant accent** (electric blue, orange, hot pink, teal) | Extra-large condensed sans (Bebas/Oswald-style) | Trainer in action, before/after, real clients |
| **Wellness coach** | Soft, earthy, grounded, calm | Sage, terracotta, stone, warm whites | Elegant serif + soft sans, generous spacing | Natural light, outdoors, textures |
| **Business coach** | Clean, authoritative, CEO-level | Navy/charcoal + white + gold/teal accent | High-contrast serif (Playfair) + clean body (Open Sans) | Professional headshots, stage/workspace |
| **Life / mindset coach** | Warm, story-driven, inviting | Blush, lavender, cream, soft gradients | Friendly serif + soft sans | Authentic lifestyle, relatable settings |
| **Beauty / lifestyle creator** | Editorial, magazine-quality | Mono + 1 fashion accent OR dopamine brights | Fashion serif (Bodoni-style) + minimal sans | Polished editorial, styled flat-lays |
| **Fitness / lifestyle influencer** | Personality-forward, aspirational | Creator's signature palette from their IG | Bold, punchy, personality-forward | Creator's face in hero, matches IG feed |

**Universal type rules:** max 2 font families · headlines 60px+ on desktop · body ≥16px · no italic body copy.

## 4. Structure by sub-audience (what each TYPE needs)

**Fitness coach / trainer → a CONVERSION site**
Home → Programs → Transformations (before/after) → About + credentials (NASM/ACE) → **embedded booking** → testimonials. Show "programs from $X." Lead magnet = free workout PDF / free week.

**Online / life / business coach → an APPLICATION FUNNEL**
Pain-led home → Work With Me (1–3 offers) → story-driven About → **qualifying application form (3–5 Qs) BEFORE the calendar** → outcome-metric testimonials. Pricing often hidden ("investment on application"). Lead magnet = quiz / scorecard / free training.

**Influencer / creator → a TWO-AUDIENCE HUB**
A **link-in-bio page** for ~90% of social traffic (mobile, action-first) + a **full site** with a **"Work With Me" page + media kit** (platform stats, audience demographics, past brand deals, rates). Keep fan CTAs ("Shop my favorites") separate from brand CTAs ("Partner with me"). Add a Shop/Favorites page for affiliate income.

---

## 5. Conversion rules that matter most

- **Hero headline = the client's pain in their words**, before any methodology. ("I help online fitness coaches hit $10K/mo without burnout" beats "I'm passionate about fitness.")
- **Cold traffic → lead magnet first** (converts 3–6× better than a cold "book a call"). **Warm traffic → Apply / Book.**
- **Qualifying form before booking** (coaches) ~doubles close rate and protects your calendar.
- **Testimonials convert when** they have full name + headshot + a specific metric ("Lost 22 lbs in 10 weeks"). Video > written.
- **80% of coaching conversions happen on touch 5+** → email capture + follow-up is mandatory.

---

## 6. The 10 conversion killers (auto-fail if present)

1. Hero talks about the coach, not the client
2. Vague transformation ("live your best life")
3. No lead magnet for cold traffic
4. Social proof on one page only
5. Stock photos
6. Slow load (each extra second ≈ −4.4% conversion)
7. More than 5–6 nav items
8. Desktop-designed, not mobile-tested
9. Aesthetics over psychology (no clear "what do I do next")
10. No email capture / no follow-up sequence

## The 3 rules that override everything

1. **Client-first hero** — name their problem, not your name.
2. **Mobile is the product** — 91–94% arrive on a phone.
3. **Social proof is infrastructure** — distributed everywhere, not decoration.
